To use vents, you need a role that has the ability to vent:

- All Impostor roles: [Impostor](/roles/imposters/imposter), [Camouflager](/roles/imposters/camouflager), [Miner](/roles/imposters/miner), [Morphling](/roles/imposters/morphling), [Phantom](/roles/imposters/phantom)
- [Engineer](/roles/crewmates/engineer)

## How to Vent

Stand next to a vent — one of your ability items will change from a barrier to a tripwire hook. Right-click it to enter the vent.

While vented, red glowing blocks will appear at all connected vents, visible even through blocks. To switch to a different vent, look at one of the red glowing blocks and left-click it.

:::tip[Yellow means ready]
When looking at a red glowing block, it will turn yellow to indicate that you can vent to it.
:::

:::note[Client-Server Delay]
Due to the delay between client and server, the red glowing blocks may not turn yellow immediately when you look at them. However, left-clicking will still work correctly, as the client sends the player's precise rotation on click.
:::

To exit the vent, right-click the vent item again.

:::caution[Vent Item Only]
Only the vent item works while venting. Clicking any other item will have no effect. Additionally, most abilities cannot be used while vented.
:::

:::note[Miner Vents]
You can also access vents created by the [Miner](/roles/imposters/miner).
:::
