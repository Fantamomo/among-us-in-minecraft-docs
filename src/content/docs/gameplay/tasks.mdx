---
title: Tasks
description: Learn how tasks work, including categories, tracking, and how impostors interact with them.
---

At the start of the game, you will be assigned a set of random tasks.

Tasks are divided into 3 categories:

- **Short:** A quick task that can be completed in a single step.
- **Long:** A more involved task that takes longer to complete or has multiple steps.
- **Common:** Short tasks that every player receives — all players will have the same common tasks.

## Tracking Your Tasks

Your tasks are listed in the scoreboard. At the start, all tasks appear in gray with a red `✖`, indicating they have not been completed yet.

Yellow markers in your locator bar show the direction of each incomplete task. When facing a task's direction, its name will appear in your action bar above the locator bar.

:::note[Task Highlight]
When you are near a task, the block you need to click to open the task menu will be highlighted in yellow.
:::

## Task States

- **Gray with a red `✖`** — Not yet started.
- **Yellow with a yellow filled circle `⬤`** — Started but not yet completed.
- **Green with a green `✔`** — Completed. The task name will appear with a strikethrough and move below all incomplete tasks in the scoreboard.

Some tasks have multiple steps that must be completed in order. Upon completing a step, a title will show how many steps remain. Once the entire task is finished, a title will display `✔ Task Completed!`

Two tasks also feature a countdown timer, which is displayed in the scoreboard.

## Impostors and Tasks

Impostors receive fake tasks that appear in their scoreboard like normal tasks. However, since they cannot actually complete them, all tasks will always be marked with a red `✖`.

:::tip[Fake Task Markers]
As an impostor, you will still see yellow markers in your locator bar and yellow outlines on nearby task blocks, helping you blend in with crewmates.
:::

## Communications Down

If communications are sabotaged, the scoreboard will no longer display any tasks. Instead, each task entry will show a dark red `[N/A] ⚠`. Task markers will also disappear from your locator bar.

However, the yellow outline on nearby task blocks remains visible and tasks can still be completed normally.

## Bossbar

If not disabled, a bossbar will be shown that fills up as more tasks are completed, giving all players a visual overview of the overall task progress.
