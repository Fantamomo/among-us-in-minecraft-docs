As the host in the lobby, you have access to several commands to manage your game:

1. `/au start` — Begins a 10-second countdown.
2. `/au settings` — Opens the settings menu, where you can customize the game.
3. `/au ban <player>` — Bans a player from the current game. They will not be able to rejoin.
4. `/au invite <player>` — Invites any online player to the current game. The invited player will receive a clickable message in chat — clicking it will add them to the game.

## Wardrobe

A wardrobe is available in the lobby. It automatically mirrors the appearance of the player viewing it.

Right-clicking the wardrobe opens a menu where you can customize:

- **Color** — Change your player color.
- **Helmet Trim** — Adjust the armor trim on your helmet.
