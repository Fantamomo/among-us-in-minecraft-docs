import {Badge, CardGrid, LinkCard, Steps} from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

Modifications are gameplay modifiers that change how a player behaves in the game.

Some provide advantages, while others introduce disadvantages or unique mechanics.

Currently, there are five modifications:

<CardGrid>
    <LinkCard title="Small" href="../small" description="Reduces the player’s size." />
    <LinkCard title="Radar" href="../radar" description="Shows the nearest player’s direction and distance." />
    <LinkCard title="Speed" href="../speed" description="Increases movement speed." />
    <LinkCard title="Torch" href="../torch" description="Grants better visibility in dark areas." />
    <LinkCard title="Laggy" href="../laggy" description="Reduces update frequency, causing delayed synchronization." />
</CardGrid>

:::caution[Experimental]
Modifications are still experimental and may lead to unfair gameplay, or exploits.

Not all maps support all modifications.
:::

:::tip[Enable modifications]
Modifications are default disabled.

To enable them:

<Steps>
    1. Open the game settings. (via `/au settings`)
    2. Enable the **Modifications** tab. (that with the `Potion` item)
    3. Click on the red glass pane to enable the modification.
</Steps>
:::
