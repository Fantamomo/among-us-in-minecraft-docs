---
title: Torch modification
description: What the torch modification does
---

The Torch modification improves visibility for the player.

When a light sabotage is active, the player can continue to see normally instead of being affected by darkness.

:::caution[Sprinting]
Players with the Torch modification are not affected by the darkness effect during a light sabotage.

Because of this, they can still sprint normally. Sprinting may reveal that they have the Torch modification to impostors, making them easier to identify and potentially increasing the risk of being killed.
:::

#### Availability
- Available to: **Crewmates only**
