---
title: Laggy modification
description: What the laggy modification does
---

The Laggy modification reduces how frequently the player’s state is synchronized.

Instead of updating every tick, updates occur only at fixed intervals (every 20 ticks), causing delayed movement updates and visual desynchronization.

The player which has this modification will experience not any lag, only other players will see the player update every second.

#### Availability
- Available to: **All players**
