---
title: Limitations of Bots
description: The limitations of bots
---

Bots are still experimental and currently in development.

Because of this, they may have bugs or unexpected behavior.

## Known limitations

- Bots can sometimes get stuck and stop moving.
- In some cases, bots may suddenly stop responding or freeze.
- Their behavior is still being improved and may not always work as expected.

We are actively working on improving bots and making them more reliable in future updates.
