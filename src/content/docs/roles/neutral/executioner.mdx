---
title: Executioner
description: The Executioner is a Neutral role that wins by getting their assigned target ejected during a meeting.
---

import {Badge} from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Badge text="Neutral" variant="tip"/>

At the start of the game, the Executioner is assigned a random player as their target.

The Executioner's only win condition is getting their target ejected during a meeting.

:::tip[Who is my target?]
The target's name is shown on the scoreboard, and their nametag appears in green above their head.
:::

:::note[Edge Cases]
The target will never be a Jester.

In the rare case where no valid target is available, the Executioner cannot win.
:::
