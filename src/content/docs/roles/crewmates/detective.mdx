title: Detective

The Detective can see the footprints of other players.

Footprints appear as particles in the player’s color, allowing the Detective to track their movements.

If a player morphs into someone else, their footprints change to match the color of the player they are disguised as.

When a player enters a vent, no new footprints are created while they are inside.
However, footprints leading up to the vent remain visible. New footprints will only appear again once the player exits the vent.

If the Camouflager activates their ability and disguises all players, any new footprints will appear in the camouflage color instead of the players’ original colors.
