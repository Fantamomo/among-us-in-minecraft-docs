title: The Damned
description: The Damned is a Crewmate role that forces the killer to instantly report their body upon death, triggering an emergency meeting.
---

import {Badge} from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Badge text="Crewmate" variant="note"/>

The Damned has no active abilities.

However, if the Damned is killed, the killer will automatically report the body, instantly triggering a meeting.

If the [Sheriff](../sheriff) kills the Damned, no automatic report is triggered and no meeting is called.
