title: Mayor
description: The Mayor is a Crewmate role with two votes during meetings, allowing them to vote twice per round.
---

<Badge text="Crewmate" variant="note"/>

The Mayor has two votes instead of one during meetings.

First, he casts his normal vote. If he wants to use his second vote, he must open the voting menu again.
He can vote for the same player again or choose a different one.

:::caution
The Mayor’s extra vote does not prevent the meeting from ending.
If he is the last player who still needs to vote, he cannot use his second vote
because the meeting ends automatically once all players have voted.
:::
