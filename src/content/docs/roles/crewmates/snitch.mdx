The Snitch is a Crewmate with no active abilities.

However, when the Snitch completes all of their tasks, the identity of the Impostor is revealed — nametags of Impostors will turn red.

If the Snitch has only one task remaining, the Impostors are warned: the Snitch’s nametag will appear yellow to the Impostor.
