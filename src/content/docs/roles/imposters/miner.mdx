The Miner is an Imposter with an additional ability: creating vents.

To create a vent, the Miner right-clicks the shovel in their hotbar.
The vent will appear at the Miner’s location shortly after the action.
The Miner must remain still during vent creation, otherwise the attempt will fail.

Other players can see the vent being created, as cracks appear in the blocks around the Miner, simulating mining activity.

Once created, the vent is visible to all players, and anyone who can use vents can immediately use it.

Each vent the Miner creates is part of their personal vent network, separate from other Miners.
All vents created by the same Miner are interconnected.
