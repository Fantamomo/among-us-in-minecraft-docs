The Phantom can enter **ghost mode**, leaving his body behind while moving freely around the map.

Other players will only see the Phantom’s body, not his ghost form.

While in ghost mode, the Phantom can still eliminate players.

Ghost mode is temporary. When the time expires, the Phantom automatically returns to his body, though he can also choose to return early.
