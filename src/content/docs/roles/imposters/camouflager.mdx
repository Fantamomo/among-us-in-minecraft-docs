The Camouflager has a special ability that temporarily changes the appearance of all players to match a single, randomly chosen player.

The target player is selected completely at random, making it unpredictable.

:::note
Other players, even imposters, are not alerted when the camouflage is activated.
It may look as if one player has morphed into another, creating confusion and hiding the Camouflager’s true identity.
:::
