---
title: Morphling
description: The Morphling is an Imposter role that can fully transform into any other player, copying their name, skin, and helmet.
---

The Morphling can transform into any other player at any time. Activating this ability opens a menu where the Morphling can choose a target to copy — including other Impostors. Upon morphing, the Morphling fully adopts the target's name, skin, and helmet.

If the [MineSkin API](../../../configuration/secrets-properties#mineskin-api-key) is configured, the skin change will animate instead of swapping instantly. The animation may not appear the first time a specific player is morphed, as their skin may not be cached yet.

Detectives will see footprints in the color of the player the Morphling has currently transformed into.

:::note[Nametags]
Other Impostors will still see a red name tag above the Morphling, but it will display the copied player's name rather than the Morphling's real one. This also applies to other roles that recolor name tags — they will still see the altered color, but not the Morphling's true identity.
:::

:::note[Reporting]
If the Morphling reports a body while morphed, the report will appear under the Morphling's own identity — not the player they have copied.
:::
