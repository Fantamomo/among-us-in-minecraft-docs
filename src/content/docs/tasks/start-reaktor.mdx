In this task, the player must start the reactor by following a sequence of lights.

**How it works:**
1. A **sequence of yellow lights** will briefly appear on the left grid.
2. Memorize the sequence and repeat it on the **right grid** by clicking the corresponding slots.
3. If the player clicks the wrong slot, the task will flash red and reset with a new sequence.
4. After correctly repeating the sequence, the next step increases the length of the sequence until the task is complete.

:::caution
Closing the inventory will cause an error and reset the task.
:::
