In this task, the player must repair a drill by repeatedly interacting with multiple malfunctioning parts.

**How it works:**
1. Some slots in the inventory are marked with **red panels** representing drill parts that need fixing.
2. Each red panel has a **hit counter** (shown as the item amount) indicating how many interactions are required to repair it.
3. Click on a red panel to reduce its counter. When the counter reaches zero, the panel turns **green**, indicating that part is repaired.
4. Continue repairing all red panels until all turn green. Once every part is repaired, the task is completed.
