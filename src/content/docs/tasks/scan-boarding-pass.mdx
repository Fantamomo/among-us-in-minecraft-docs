Type: <Badge text="Common" variant="note" />

In this task, the player must scan a boarding pass by placing it into the correct slot and waiting for the progress to complete.

**How it works:**
1. Place the **boarding pass** item into the target slot.
2. The task will automatically begin scanning the pass.
3. A series of **status indicators** (blue panes) will gradually turn **green** as the scan progresses.
4. Once all indicators are green, the task is complete.

:::caution
Removing the boarding pass from the target slot before the scan finishes will cause progress to reset.
Closing the inventory also resets the task.
:::
