In this task, the player must transfer data from one terminal to another.

**How it works:**
1. Approach the **data terminal** and press the **start button** to begin.
2. A progress bar will fill as the data transfers.
3. Once the transfer is complete, go to the **upload location** to finish the task.
4. The task consists of **two steps**: starting the transfer and uploading it at the destination.

:::caution
Closing the inventory before the progress completes will reset the task. Progress is only saved after the upload step is completed.
:::
