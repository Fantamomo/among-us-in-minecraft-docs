| <Badge text="Long" variant="danger" /> | `clear_asteroids` |

In this task, the player must destroy incoming asteroids.

Asteroids appear randomly in the task interface. Click on each asteroid to shoot it. A counter at the bottom shows how many asteroids are left to destroy.

The task is complete once the required number of asteroids has been cleared.
