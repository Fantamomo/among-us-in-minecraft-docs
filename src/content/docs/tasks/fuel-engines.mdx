In this task, the player must fuel the engines by shuttling fuel between the refuel station and the engines.

**How it works:**
- Start at the refuel station to fill your fuel bucket.
- Then move to the engine location and empty the bucket to fuel it.
- Repeat this process for all engine stations until the task is complete.
